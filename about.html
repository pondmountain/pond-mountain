Pond Mountain Cattle Co.
Just a little bit about this wonderful place.

What can you say about such a beautiful place? Well...probably a heck of a lot!
Nestled in the absolute and I mean absolute mostest north westernest part of North Carolina in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains and hell and gone from, well... anywhere!
Up here we take life at a little different pace then it seems most other people do...wide   ass   open!

I reckon it is the only way we can keep from freezing! You sit around here to long and your butt cheeks might get frozen to something.

You see the view from the Pond Mountain Ponderosa is a sight to be hold! That is for about 6 weeks...
Other wise if you don't like the color white then I would advise you to stay home!
But don't worry if you do decide to brave the trip to come see us at any time other then July,

and you find that your vehicle can not gain the proper traction due to a foot thick sheet of ice
we do have a bulldozer that can get you out!
We will require that a waiver is signed prior to tow...

But once you are up here, and you figure out which snow drift is actually the house,
we can do lots of wonderful things, like, cut down some trees, chop some wood, ice fish, chop more wood, fix fence,
figure out where the tractor went, chase some cows, chop wood, or we can just sit back with a hot tottie and watch
Mollie B's Polka hour on RFD-TV...that is if the wind hasn't blown away the satellite dish...
Can't wait to see ya! Our axes are always sharp, should decide to drop in!
